Setting Up MetaMask Wallet

Step 1: Install MetaMask Extension

MetaMask is a browser extension that allows you to interact with the Ethereum blockchain.

  1. Visit the MetaMask website
  2. Click "Install MetaMask for Chrome" (or your preferred browser)
  3. Click "Add to Chrome" and confirm by clicking "Add extension"

Step 2: Create a New Wallet

Set up your new MetaMask wallet by following these instructions:

  1. Click the MetaMask icon in your browser's extension area
  2. Click "Get Started" and then "Create a Wallet"
  3. Create a password for your wallet
  4. You will be shown a Secret Recovery Phrase (12 words) - write this down and keep it in a secure place
  5. Verify your Secret Recovery Phrase by selecting the words in the correct order

Step 3: Connect to E-Vault

Once your MetaMask wallet is set up, you can connect it to our E-Vault platform:

  1. Ensure you're logged into MetaMask
  2. Return to the E-Vault platform
  3. Click the "Connect MetaMask" button in the top-right corner
  4. A MetaMask popup will appear asking for permission to connect
  5. Click "Connect" to authorize the connection
  6. After successful connection, you can access E-Vault features using your Aadhar UID and connected wallet

Importance to the Indian Judiciary System

Security & Data Integrity

Blockchain technology ensures tamper-proof storage of legal records, preventing unauthorized modifications and maintaining the integrity of case documents. This builds trust in the judicial process and eliminates concerns about document tampering.

Transparency & Accessibility

E-Vault provides transparent access to case information for authorized parties while maintaining appropriate privacy controls. This ensures that clients, lawyers, and judges can easily access necessary documents, reducing delays and improving judicial efficiency.

Real-time tracking of case progress enables better resource management, allowing the judiciary to identify bottlenecks and allocate resources more effectively to improve overall system performance.
